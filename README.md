# Regional Science & Regional Economics — Living Research Briefing

An auto-updating briefing of the latest papers across 15 regional science,
urban/spatial economics, and rural/land journals.

**Live briefing:** https://yangcheng258.github.io/Regional_Science_Briefing/ (GitHub Pages serves `index.html`)

- `index.html` — interactive briefing (search, filter by journal/keyword, deep-read shortlist, DOI links)
- `briefing.xlsx` — same data as a sortable spreadsheet
- `data/papers.json` — the growing archive (one record per paper; `added` = date it entered the archive)
- `data/overview.json` — themes, deep-read picks
- `scripts/` — build scripts that render the HTML and XLSX from the data
- `UPDATE_INSTRUCTIONS.md` — the runbook the weekly automated Claude session follows

Updated weekly by a scheduled task in Claude Cowork. Abstracts are included where
openly available (Springer, SAGE, OUP, Wiley-via-OUCI, Elsevier-econ-via-RePEc);
for the rest, each entry links to its DOI.

Journals covered: Journal of Regional Science, Papers in Regional Science, Annals of
Regional Science, Regional Science and Urban Economics, Regional Studies, Spatial
Economic Analysis, International Regional Science Review, Regional Science Policy &
Practice, Journal of Urban Economics, Journal of Economic Geography, Economic
Geography, Environment and Planning A, Journal of Rural Studies, Land Use Policy,
Growth and Change.
